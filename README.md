Sales & Profit Dashboard

This repository contains an interactive Sales & Profit Dashboard designed to visualize key business metrics including total sales, total profit, sales by category, top customers, and geographic sales distribution. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview to help businesses make informed decisions.

Features

Total Sales & Profit: Displays overall sales and profit figures.

Profit by Year: Visualizes yearly profit trends across different product categories (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology).

Sales by Category: Bar chart showing total sales for individual product categories.

Top 5 Customers Profit: Highlights the top customers contributing the highest profit.

Sales by Month: Monthly sales trends for better temporal analysis.

Customer Count: Donut chart showing customer distribution across years.

Sales by State: Geographic map displaying sales distribution across U.S. states.

Filters

The dashboard includes interactive filters to customize the data view:

Category: Filter by product categories (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology).

Year: Filter data by year (2021–2024).

Month: Filter data by month (January–December).

Technologies Used

Data Visualization: Interactive charts and maps

Dashboard Design: User-friendly interface for easy insights

Data Source: Sales and profit data for products, customers, and regions

Benefits

Quickly identify high-performing products and customers

Track yearly and monthly profit trends

Understand geographic distribution of sales

Make data-driven business decisions
